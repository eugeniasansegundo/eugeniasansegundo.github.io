Dr. Eugenia San Segundo

Distinguished Researcher @ Department of Spanish Language & General Linguistics @ UNED Madrid

About me

Since March 2020 I work as Distinguished Researcher (Beatriz Galindo Junior) in the Department of Spanish Language & General Linguistics at the National University of Distance Education (UNED), Madrid.

I teach Applied Linguistics (Degree in Spanish language & literature) and an open teaching course on Forensic Phonetics (more information coming soon).

My research interests include: Forensic Speech Science, methods in Forensic Voice Comparison, likelihood-ratio approaches, formant dynamics, voice quality production and perception, and the study of twins and related speakers.
